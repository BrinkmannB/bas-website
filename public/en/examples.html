Practical Examples of AI Governance

Projects that demonstrate how responsible AI strengthens transparency, ethics, and human-centred policy.

Case 1: Fair Housing Allocation

A municipality used algorithms to improve fairness in housing allocation. Through an AI impact assessment and fairness audit, bias was detected and corrected — resulting in more transparent and trusted decision-making.

Case 2: Risk-based Inspections

A national inspectorate applied predictive AI for prioritising inspections. A governance framework ensured explainability and proportionality, strengthening public accountability and trust.

Case 3: AI Ethics in Finance

A financial institution requested guidance in drafting an AI Ethics Code. The result: practical rules for data use, transparency, and human oversight — aligned with the EU AI Act.
