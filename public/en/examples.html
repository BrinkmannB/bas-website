Practical Examples of AI Governance

Projects where responsible AI use contributes to transparency, ethics, and human-centered policy.

Case 1: AI in Housing Allocation

A municipality used algorithms to distribute housing applications more fairly. Through an impact assessment and fairness analysis, bias was detected and corrected.

Result: fairer policy, greater transparency, and increased public trust.

Case 2: Risk-Based Inspections

An inspection authority applied predictive AI to prioritize oversight. With a governance framework and regular evaluations, explainability and proportionality were ensured.

Result: decisions could be better justified to both citizens and Parliament.

Case 3: AI Ethics in the Financial Sector

A financial institution sought advice on developing an AI ethics code.

Result: clear guidelines for data use, transparency, and human oversight — aligned with European regulations such as the AI Act.
