Who we are

Dutch Governance Advisory (DGA) helps governments, institutions and companies to use AI responsibly — transparent, explainable and aligned with public values. We merge governance, ethics and policy into one practice that ensures AI serves people and society.

AI governance is not bureaucracy — it is moral leadership. DGA enables decision-makers to translate ethical principles into practical frameworks that stand up to public scrutiny and future regulation.

Our approach

We work independently, analytically and with a human touch. Our process combines risk analysis, ethical review and governance design, tailored to the context of public administration and regulated sectors.

  • ✔ Governance & risk management frameworks
  • ✔ Compliance and ethical assessments
  • ✔ Impact assessments and policy advice

Our mission

We believe AI governance should not slow innovation, but steer it. True progress means aligning technology with justice, accountability and trust. Dutch Governance Advisory stands for transparent, explainable and value-driven AI — for people and society.
